About One or Zero

One or Zero is a computing hardware website built in the spirit of collaboration. The core of the One or Zero system is built on top of the popular MediaWiki application, known for it's use by Wikipedia. MediaWiki at our core enables us to leverage an extensive dataset of product information to power our One or Zero Charts tool.

As of now, anything other than MediaWiki functionaility was designed developed by Cyrus Cuenca. The wiki is made possible by our generous contributers.
